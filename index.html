Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Pictionary ("the Game"), developed by BEDIGITECH, handles information when you use the App.

1. Information Handling

Pictionary does not require user registration or an account.

The App asks you to enter a player name before joining a multiplayer game. This player name is used only during the active game session so other players can identify you. The player name is not permanently stored by BEDIGITECH after the game ends.

The App does not intentionally collect or store personal information such as:

  • Email addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • Home addresses
  • Government identification numbers
  • Payment information
  • Precise location information

2. Multiplayer Gameplay

The App provides real-time multiplayer gameplay using online servers. Information such as player names, drawings, chat messages, scores, and game events is processed only while a game is active to enable multiplayer functionality. This information is not permanently stored by BEDIGITECH once the game session ends.

3. Firebase Services

The App uses the following Firebase services:

  • Firebase Analytics to understand app usage, improve performance, and identify crashes or technical issues.
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) to send optional push notifications about updates or important announcements.

Firebase may collect anonymous technical information such as device information and app usage statistics in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.

4. Push Notifications

Push notifications are optional. You may disable notifications at any time through your device settings.

5. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Analytics
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM)

6. Data Security

Reasonable technical and organizational measures are used to protect information transmitted during multiplayer gameplay. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is completely secure, and absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

7. Your Choices

  • You may choose any display name when joining a game.
  • You may disable push notifications through your device settings.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
