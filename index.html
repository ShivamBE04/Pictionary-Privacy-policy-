Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Pictionary ("the App"), developed by BEDIGITECH, collects, uses, and protects information when you use the App.

1. Information We Collect

The App may collect the following information:

  • Player name used during multiplayer gameplay.
  • Chat messages exchanged during game sessions.
  • Gameplay information such as drawings, scores, room codes, and game events.
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) registration tokens used for push notifications.
  • Anonymous analytics and diagnostic information through Firebase Analytics.

2. How We Use Information

The collected information is used to:

  • Provide real-time multiplayer gameplay.
  • Synchronize game sessions between players.
  • Display player names, drawings, and scores.
  • Improve app performance, stability, and user experience.
  • Analyze gameplay and application usage.
  • Send optional push notifications using Firebase Cloud Messaging.

3. Push Notifications

The App uses Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) to send optional push notifications. These notifications may include game announcements, updates, or other important information.

Notification permission is requested by the App on supported Android devices. You may disable notifications at any time through your device settings.

4. Analytics

The App uses Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with the App, improve features, monitor stability, and enhance the overall experience. Analytics information is collected anonymously and does not directly identify individual users.

5. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase Analytics
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM)

6. Data Storage and Security

Gameplay data is processed during active multiplayer sessions. Reasonable technical and organizational measures are used to protect data during transmission. We do not intentionally retain personal information longer than necessary.

7. Personal Information

The App does not require user registration and does not intentionally collect personally identifiable information such as:

  • Email addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • Home addresses
  • Precise location information

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so that appropriate action can be taken.

9. Data Retention

Gameplay information is generally retained only for the duration of active multiplayer sessions, except where anonymous analytics or legal obligations require limited retention.

10. Your Choices

You may:

  • Disable push notifications from your device settings.
  • Manage your privacy preferences through your Google account where applicable.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
